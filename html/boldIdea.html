Overview

A few months ago I began volunteering with a nonprofit in Dallas, Texas called Bold Idea. Their goal is to educate kids in computer science through workshops and after school programs. The group I was a part of was responsible for curriculum design and development for the program's student guidebook.

My Role

1. Propose and research potential topics and/or activities.
2. Draft student-facing guidebook pages (examples below) and tips for in-person mentors helping students.

Challenges

1. Working remotely with a seven hour time difference from everyone else was difficult at times, but with weekly video meetings, messages and online task trackers, communication issues were worked out.
2. Working with students from Spain in the same age range helped me be aware of an appropriate level of difficulty for explanations.
3. Starting from (mostly) scratch. Because most things having to do with the organization are very new, it was difficult to know what was needed at times but I found a good balance of working independently with occasional feedback.
